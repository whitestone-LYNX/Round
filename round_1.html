Round

Play your part once. Round hands it back to you, delayed, so the next entrance has someone to play against — you, a phrase ago.

Wear headphones before you turn this on. Round sends your delayed playing to your speakers in real time. If a speaker plays it back into an open microphone, the sound loops on itself and builds into a screech. Headphones keep the loop out of the mic entirely.
